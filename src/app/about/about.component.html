About The Community

  • Since 2008, the homeowners of Mill Creek have managed the community affairs through a volunteer homeowner elected board of directors, consisting of five homeowner representatives. As fellow homeowners, we share the same passion to ensure that our investment in our homes is managed properly while protecting and preserving the rights of our neighbors within the community.

    This site contains useful information for homeowners of Mill Creek that includes covenants, contact information, modification request forms, mailbox replacement, tennis court reservations, and much more by visiting the pages within this site. For further information, please view our Mill Creek Mission Statement section.
